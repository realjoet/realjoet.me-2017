⚡️ Joe Toscano ( yo@realjoet.me )

Yo! My name is Joe. People on the Internet tend to refer to me as @realjoet.

I have experiences and education in a variety of fields, all of which synergistically improve my ability to creatively solve problems. I work to make experiences that are so useful, usable, and compelling, they can’t help but be adopted and move us forward.

I'm currently traveling the world speaking about design ethics in the era of automation and working to develop my 501(c)3 non-profit, Design Good.

Design Good's mission is to (1) help improve both data and technical literacy within the general population, (2) enable technologists to create products that not only respond to informed consumer demand but also create positive social impact while increasing revenue, and (3) enable policymakers to ask the right questions so they can understand what happening and create regulation that not only protects consumers but also strengthens national security and enables businesses to operate successfully in our global economy.

Think of it like the Better Business Bureau (BBB), but better looking and for the Internet.

So far I've been invited to 19 different states or nations across four continents, including private consultations with large corps such as SAP, IBM, and R/GA, among others, and keynote presentations to audiences of global leaders. It has also led to a publishing deal with Penguin Randomhouse. And as of November 2018 you can order my book, Automating Humanity.

My goal in life? Create the future. And do it ethically.
