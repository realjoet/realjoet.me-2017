Joe Toscano { yo@realjoet.me }

Yo! My name is Joe. People tell me I'm an interesting dude.

I have experiences and education in a variety of fields, all of which synergistically improve my ability to creatively solve problems. I work to make experiences that are so useful, usable, and compelling, they can’t help but be adopted and move us forward.

I'm currently traveling the world speaking about my upcoming book series, Designing Intelligence. The series is my attempt to layout an ethical design thinking approach to designing in the era of ambient computing.

So far I've spoken in 16 different states across three continents, including private consultations with large corps such as SAP, IBM, and Google, among others, and keynote presentations to audiences of global leaders.

My larger goal in life? Create the future.
